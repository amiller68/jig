Move `derive_worker_name` and `sanitize_worker_name` out of `issues/naming.rs` and into `git/branch.rs` as methods on `Branch`.

Leave `extract_linear_identifier` in `issues/naming.rs` — that's Linear-specific and thing-1 owns it.
